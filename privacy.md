
# Jaguar Bot Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Jaguar Bot ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Bot.

## 1. Information We Collect

Jaguar Bot may collect and process the following information:

- Discord User IDs
- Discord Server IDs
- Discord Channel IDs
- Discord Role IDs
- Command usage data
- Configuration settings required for the Bot to function

Jaguar Bot does not collect passwords, payment information, or private messages unless a feature explicitly requires it.

## 2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used to:

- Provide and operate the Bot's features.
- Save server settings and configurations.
- Improve performance and reliability.
- Prevent abuse and ensure security.

## 3. Data Sharing

Jaguar Bot does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Information may only be shared if required by law or to protect the security and integrity of the service.

## 4. Data Storage

Data is stored only for as long as necessary to provide the Bot's services.

Server administrators may request the removal of their server's stored data where applicable.

## 5. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Third-Party Services

Jaguar Bot operates on Discord and may rely on services provided by Discord. Your use of Discord is also governed by Discord's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of Jaguar Bot after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 8. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the Jaguar Bot support team through the official support server.
