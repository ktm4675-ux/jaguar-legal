Jaguar Bot does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except where required by law.

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide Jaguar Bot's functionality.

5. Security

Reasonable security measures are implemented to protect stored information. However, no online service is completely secure.

6. Third-Party Services

Jaguar Bot operates on Discord and may rely on services provided by Discord. Their own policies also apply.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated without prior notice. Continued use of Jaguar Bot indicates acceptance of the latest version.
